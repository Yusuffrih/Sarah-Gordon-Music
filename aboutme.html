Welcome!

Hi there, my name is Sarah Gordon. I am 28 years old and from the beautiful city of Dublin. I love to sing more than anything in the world. Something that makes me really happy is being able to use my talent to bring joy to other people, especially on on special occasions like weddings. There's something really great and very emotional about singing a song during a ceremony of marriage. Nearly all music is about love and what says love more than a wedding?
